PIZZA RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 kg plain flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 7 g sachet of instant yeast

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Sift the flour into a large bowl and stir in the salt and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the warm water, olive oil, and yeast.
  4. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry ingredients and stir to form a dough.
  5. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise for 30 minutes.
  7. Punch down the dough and divide it into two. Roll each piece into a circle.
  8. Add your favorite toppings and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
